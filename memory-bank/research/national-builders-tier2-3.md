# National Builders Research: Tier 2 & Tier 3

Research date: March 2026. Tech assessment and contact identification for national builders. Goal: identify tech maturity (opportunity for Finch) and specific people to reach for demos.

## Key Finding: Envision Options (by Zonda) is the Incumbent

Multiple builders on this list use **Envision Options** (edc2.envisionoptions.com), a Zonda/NewHomeSource product. It is a configurator/wishlist tool -- buyers browse options with photos and pricing, save favorites, and prepare for design studio appointments. **It does NOT provide visual rendering or AI visualization.** It organizes the selection process but does not change buyer behavior. This is Finch's opening: Envision tells you what you can pick. Finch shows you what it looks like.

Builders on Envision: Taylor Morrison, Tri Pointe Homes, KB Home, M/I Homes (their own "Envision Design Tool" -- same platform or similar).

## Competitive Intelligence Notes

- **Envision Options (Zonda)** is the dominant configurator platform. Used by Taylor Morrison, Tri Pointe, KB Home, and likely M/I Homes. It is a selection/wishlist tool -- NOT a visualizer. This is industry standard. Finch is additive to (not competitive with) Envision.
- **Roomored (Interior Logic Group)** is the only real visualization competitor found. Used by Shea Homes. 3D rendering on actual floorplans. Requires 3D modeling (expensive/slow setup vs Finch's photo-based approach).
- **Matterport** is used for virtual tours of physical design studios (Mattamy), not for interactive selection visualization.
- **Kitchen 360 (Toll Brothers)** is a proprietary VR app -- limited deployment, requires VR headset.
- **No builder on this list uses AI-generated visualization.** Finch has a clear category-creation opportunity.
